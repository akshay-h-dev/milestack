# Milestack

## The Problem

In modern workplaces, teams often rely on multiple disconnected tools for communication, task management, and progress tracking. This fragmentation leads to significant inefficiencies: miscommunication, duplicated efforts, missed deadlines, and a lack of clarity on individual contributions and overall project health.

## The Solution: Milestack

Milestack is an all-in-one project collaboration tool designed to solve this problem by unifying the essential functions of team collaboration into a single, intuitive platform. It streamlines workflows, enhances visibility, and fosters accountability.

### Core Features

*   **Project Management:** Organize your work into distinct projects, each with its own dedicated workspace.
*   **Kanban Dashboard:** Visualize your workflow with a drag-and-drop task board to manage tasks through "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done" stages.
*   **Milestones:** Define and track major project goals to keep your team aligned and focused on the bigger picture.
*   **Real-time Chat:** Communicate with your team in dedicated threads, keeping conversations organized and contextually relevant.
*   **Activity Feed:** Maintain a clear and transparent log of all contributions and actions taken within your projects.
*   **Team Management:** Easily invite and manage your team members from a central location.
*   **AI-Powered Assistance:** Leverage AI to filter important notifications and summarize chat histories, reducing noise and helping you catch up quickly.
